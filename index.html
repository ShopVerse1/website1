Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How can I track my order?

A: You can track your order using the order ID provided in your confirmation email on our Track Order page.

Q: What payment methods do you accept?

A: We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and digital wallets through Razorpay.

Q: How long does shipping take?

A: Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping takes 2-3 business days.

Q: Can I cancel my order?

A: Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. After that, you can return the item once received.

A: Currently, we only ship within India. International shipping will be available soon.
